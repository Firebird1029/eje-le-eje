Eje le Eje

Ethopian nonprofit

About

Eje le Eje Ethiopia is a nonprofit that helps underprivileged children from single-mother households in Ethiopia access basic education. We aim to break cycles of intergenerational poverty using education and encourage active community participation to allow individuals to make a real impact in their local community.

What We Do

We sponsor children between ages 3-5 to go to local private kindergartens by paying their school fees. After kindergarten, we will ensure students continue their education at local public schools by partnering with the Education Bureau at the local level and covering the cost of school supplies and uniforms. We will also organize skill training programs for their mothers in collaboration with other local nonprofits and NGOs where trainees can get seed money to open their businesses.

What Makes Us Different

We believe that community members in big urban cities in Ethiopia could be a primary source of support for underprivileged and vulnerable children to get access to basic education through sponsorship and other donations. Our non-profit will be working to leverage this untapped domestic potential in local communities and ensure that it is a community-led, and community-owned enterprise.We believe that active participation and involvement of community members in our projects will increase the number of sponsors and raise adequate funds to ensure the sustainability of the nonprofit.
